Who We Are

Ganesh Plywood Industries has been catering the varied requirements of Furniture and Construction industries, ever since its incorporation. The company is primarily engaged in the manufacturing and supplying of Plywoods, Commercial Block Boards, Flush Doors, Core Veneer and Wooden Doors all across India. These products are made from the high-grade wood in tune with the modern interior trends. Further, our products have higher strength and impact resistance. Further, these products have been fully treated to resist termites for lifetime, which is one of their major attributes. We can provide our products in desired quantities and on immediate basis as well.

Based in Uttar Pradesh (India), Ganesh Plywood Industries is known as a key Manufacturer and Supplier of Marine Plywood and Wooden Doors in India. To deliver nothing but the best to the clients, the company keeps a check on all the levels of manufacturing and packing.
